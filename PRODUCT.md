# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Local business owners — contractors, trades, service businesses, brick-and-mortar operators — who know they need marketing but have been burned by agencies that overpromise and underdeliver. They come to AR Strategies because they're tired of wasting money on ads that don't ring the phone and want an agency that speaks their language, shows real results, and handles everything so they can run their business.

Context: They're usually on desktop during the workday, evaluating options between jobs. They're making a high-stakes decision (this is their revenue pipeline) and need to feel confident before handing over their marketing budget.

## Product Purpose

AR Strategies is a done-for-you marketing agency for local businesses. We audit, manage, and scale their advertising — Google Ads, Meta Ads, content — and handle the full pipeline from lead to phone call. The site is the primary sales surface: it must convert skeptical visitors into weekly-list subscribers and work-with-us applications by demonstrating competence, proving results, and projecting premium operational confidence.

Success: A local business owner lands on the site, feels the agency is sharp and real (not another template agency), reads enough to trust, and either joins the free weekly fix list or applies to work with us. There is no free-audit offer: the audit could not be delivered reliably, so the site never promises one.

## Brand Personality

**Sharp, operator-led, results-first.** We've been in the trenches. We fix what's broken. No fluff, no stock photos of handshakes, no vague promises. The design itself is the first proof point: if the agency's own site is premium, intentional, and unlike every other agency template, the visitor trusts that same rigor will be applied to their marketing.

3 words: **Sharp. Operational. Distinctive.**

Emotional arc for visitors: curious (the design is unlike any agency they've seen) → impressed (this feels premium, competent) → trusting (the proof, the clarity, the tone — this agency delivers).

## Anti-references

- Generic WordPress agency templates with stock photos, blue nav bars, and hero sliders
- SaaS landing page clichés: gradient-glass cards, hero metrics templates (big numbers + small labels), iterated icon-grid sections
- Bootstrap-era agency sites with "ABOUT / SERVICES / CONTACT" as the entire visual language
- Anything that could be mistaken for a theme forest template
- AI-slop design patterns: cream/sand backgrounds, gradient text, identical card grids, tiny eyebrow kickers above every section, numbered section markers as default scaffolding

## Design Principles

1. **Show, don't tell.** The site's own design quality is the first proof of competence. Before reading a word, the visitor knows this agency is different.

2. **Visual evidence over decorative ornament.** Every visual element — the neural network hero, the typography hierarchy, the spacing — must earn its place by communicating something about agency capability. Nothing purely decorative.

3. **Dark confidence.** The black-first palette signals premium intent, not "dark mode because it's cool." It's a deliberate brand choice: we're not another cheerful local agency. We're the sharp operator.

4. **Engineering attention.** Layout, motion, and typography are weapons in an attention economy. Every scroll decision is intentional. The site must be technically impressive — WebGL, Canvas 2D at 60fps, smooth motion — because technical execution IS the product messaging for a performance marketing agency.

5. **Local without looking local.** We serve local businesses, but the site must never look small-town. Premium, ambitious, cosmopolitan — the agency these business owners wish they had, now finally accessible.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- WCAG AA minimum across all surfaces
- `prefers-reduced-motion` respected throughout: all animations have reduced/static alternatives
- Keyboard navigable: all interactive elements focusable, forms accessible
- Semantic HTML: proper heading hierarchy, landmark roles, ARIA labels where needed
- Color contrast: brand orange on dark meets 4.5:1 for text; light mode tokens verified for readability
- Both dark and light modes fully operational with theme persistence via localStorage
